Definition of Service
Service Declaration

This service is currently available only to Omani shareholders and non-Omani shareholders, whose identity is validated by electronic authentication.

Those not covered by this service must download an investor registration form and read their terms and conditions. The documents mentioned in the application form will be attached to the application.

  • Service Channels

    • Yes,Listed companies in MCD can follow up their accounts and obtain various services and reports by registering in the MCD website. Registration is done on site with some basic documents provided

  • Required Documents
    • The following documents should be attached with the application form:
      Section 1: Tax Return Form :
      • The tax return form must be downloaded, completed and signed. (download Individual Tax Residency)*
      • An electronic copy must be attached with the remaining documents required during filling of the electronic application. No application will be accepted without attachingthe tax return form.
      • To find out more, or to answer any questions, please contact the Capital Market Authority and Secretariat General for Taxation.
      Section 2: Identity Documents:
      A) Individuals (Omani Shareholders):
      • Adults: Copy of ID card or Passport
      • Minors (Under 18 years old):
      • - Copy of passport or birth certificate (issued by the Civil Status).
      • - Copy of the guardians’ Identity.
        • B) Individuals (Non-Omani Shareholders):
        1) Adults:
      • Residents of the Sultanate of Oman:
      • - Copy of resident card.
      • - Copy of passport.
      • Non-Residents in Oman:
      • - Copy of passport.
      • - Ratification of the form and attachments by the competent authorities.
        • 2) Minors (Under 18 years old):
      • Residents of the Sultanate of Oman:
      • - Copy of resident card.
      • - Copy of passport or birth certificate.
      • - A copy of the resident's ID card or passport.
      • Non-Residents in Oman:
      • - Copy of passport or birth certificate.
      • - Copy of the guardian's identity.
      • - Ratification of the form and attachments by the competent authorities.
      Section 3: A copy of the proof of the bank account
